💡 Pro Money-Saving Hacks for Friends

01

License Problem? AC Mo Bus / OTDC Bus Hack!

Agar kisi ke paas driving license nahi hai, toh scooty rent nahi ho payegi. Fret not! Mo Bus Route 53 AC (Puri stand to Konark) costs only **₹50 per person** one-way (₹600 round-trip for 6 friends!). Alternatively, book the **OTDC Government Sightseeing AC Bus** for **₹350-₹400 per seat** round trip, which is fully legal, safe, and comfortable!

02

Chilika Lake Boating Counter Warning

When you reach Satapada (Chilika Lake), local brokers/touts will surround you, offering boat tours for ₹4,000+. **Do NOT book with them.** Head straight to the official **OTDC Government Booking Counter**. The rates are fixed, government-regulated, and they charge only ₹1,200 to ₹1,800 for the entire boat (up to 8 people!).

03

Mahaprasad Lunch at Anand Bazar

Plan your lunch inside Jagannath Temple at the famous **Anand Bazar** (the world's largest food court). The **Abadha Mahaprasad** (cooked in earthen pots) is highly nutritious, extremely tasty, and costs only ₹80-₹120 per head. It easily feeds 7 people abundantly for a very low budget!

🍽️ Food & Alcohol Budget Rate Card

Puri & BBSR Local Rates

Puri aur Bhubaneswar mein khane-peene aur alcohol ka average rates aur strict rules:

V

Best Pure Veg Food Rates

🟢 **Abadha Mahaprasad (Puri Temple):** ₹80 - ₹120 per thali (Anand Bazar me full traditional meal cooked in earthen pots. Extremely pious, pure & filling!).

🟢 **Odia Veg Thali / Dalma Thali:** ₹90 - ₹150 in local restaurants (includes Dalma stew, Rice, crushed badis, sweet khatta chutney).

🟢 **Chena Poda Dessert:** ₹250 - ₹350 per kg (Odia sweet baked cottage cheesecake. Must try! Approx ₹30-₹50 per slice).

N

Best Non-Veg Food Rates

🔴 **Chilika Crab & Jumbo Prawns:** ₹250 - ₹400 per plate of Crab Masala or Prawn Curry (Satapada boating side or Puri beach side restaurants - fresh catch and exceptionally delicious!).

🔴 **Odia Mutton Curry (Mansa Jholo):** ₹220 - ₹320 per plate (tender mutton slow cooked in light potato gravy, highly popular at local dhabas & restaurants in Puri).

🔴 **Puri Sea Fish Curry (Machha Jholo):** ₹120 - ₹180 per plate (fresh fried Pomfret or Rohu with steam rice).

A

Alcohol Rates & Strict Rules

⚠️ **STRICT WARNING:** Puri ek holy temple town hai. **Puri Golden Beach par ya Jagannath Temple area me alcohol/beer peena strictly illegal aur prohibited hai.** Heavy fine aur police action/jail ho sakti hai! Drink only inside licensed bars or your private hotel rooms.

🍻 **Beer Price (Govt. OFF Shop):** ₹140 - ₹190 per 650ml bottle (Kingfisher, Tuborg, Bira). In local licensed beach bars/cafes: ₹220 - ₹320.

🥃 **Whiskey & Rum (Govt. OFF Shop):** ₹500 - ₹900 full bottle 750ml (Royal Stag, Blenders Pride, Magic Moments, Old Monk).
